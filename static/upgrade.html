What is Stripe?

Stripe is a service that lets programmers not have to worry about how banking works when we want to build web services. You can think of it kind of like paypal, where you send Stripe your card details, and then Stripe sends me money. It means I never see your cards details, and you don't have to trust me with them, which makes sense when it comes to the internet. It also means I don't have to worry about how to safely store them, or transmit them, or all the other worries that come with information as sensitive as credit card details. You do have to trust Stripe, but lots of people already do, so you probably can. If you'd rather not, why not leave something in the tip jar instead? A years subscription is only 12 dollars 😉
